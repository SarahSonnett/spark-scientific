Services

What Spark Scientific can do for you

Every engagement ends with something you can use — a report, a deck, a tool, or a stronger proposal.

Scientific Data Analysis

What’s really going on in your numbers? Rigorous statistical analysis separates true patterns from noise — and tells you exactly how much confidence each answer deserves.

Polished Reports & Slide Decks

Your results as finished, shareable documents: a clearly written report or a ready-to-present slide deck, with visuals that make the story obvious at a glance — deliverables your team can circulate, reuse, and build on.

Scientific Software Development

Custom tools and automated pipelines that turn hours of manual data work into a button press — documented, dependable, and yours to keep using long after the engagement ends.

Science Communication & Talks

Talks, workshops, and public events that make complex science click for any audience — from classrooms to conference stages, honed by years of teaching and outreach.

Grant Proposal Support

A scientist’s eye on your proposal before the reviewers see it — sharpening the science, the story, and the structure, informed by firsthand experience writing and winning grants.

About

A scientist in your corner

Spark Scientific is led by a practicing astrophysicist with more than a decade of research experience, including nearly ten years working on NASA space missions. That work demands exactly what your data does: careful statistical analysis, robust software, clear scientific interpretation, and the ability to coordinate across disciplines and communicate to any audience.

With a B.S. in Physics and Mathematics, an M.S. and Ph.D. in Astronomy, more than three years of collegiate teaching, and experience writing and managing science grants, she brings the full scientific toolkit to real-world problems — and a genuine passion for helping organizations think like scientists.
